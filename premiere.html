Adobe Premiere Pro is a timeline-based video editing software application developed by Adobe Systems as published as part of the Adobe Creative Cloud licensing program. First launched in 2003, Adobe Premiere Pro is a successor of Adobe Premiere. It is geared towards professional video editing , while its sibling, Adobe Premiere Elements, targets the consumer market.

I switched to Premiere Pro only a few months ago. Prior to that, I had 2.5 years of experience with Vegas Pro 13.0 but at some point, I decided that if I want to advance with my video editing skills, I need to switch to Adobe Premiere Pro because it gives more tools and possibilities. In addition, Adobe Premiere Pro has a workflow with Adobe After Effects, which allows working with special effects. Now I have, for the most part, mastered Premiere Pro and am capable of doing even more than I did in Vegas Pro 13.
